About Us

Founded in 2018, Sealpack Enterprises is a leading innovator and manufacturer of eco-conscious, semi-rigid and rigid food packaging solutions in India. We specialize in customized sustainable packaging designed to meet the specific needs of industries including food delivery, dairy, sweets, bakery, QSR, and fresh produce.

Our product catalog features 250+ SKUs, crafted with high precision using advanced German and Japanese technologies. With a robust distribution network of over 500 partners across India and abroad, we ensure timely delivery of hygienic and high-performance packaging across six key segments:

  • Takeaway Foods & Restaurant
  • Agro Products

Sealpack Enterprises Driving Global Sustainability

Our core lies in continuous innovation, modern manufacturing technologies, and purposeful product design that ensures safety, performance, and sustainability.
We don’t just create packaging — we engineer solutions that protect your products, reduce environmental impact, and reflect your brand values. From biodegradable materials to reusable and recyclable packaging formats, our R&D team works relentlessly to offer smart alternatives that benefit both businesses and the planet.
Our commitment to excellence is backed by cutting-edge machinery, global safety standards, and a vision to create packaging that goes beyond aesthetics — it’s about functionality, eco-efficiency, and long-term value. Whether it’s the food industry, pharma, or consumer goods, we deliver packaging that meets regulatory compliance while promoting green responsibility.

Our Eco-Driven Manufacturing Process

  • Waste Optimization: We use fully recyclable and post-consumer plastics, and repurpose scrap materials in a closed-loop system to maximize resource efficiency and reduce our carbon footprint.
  • Energy-Efficient Operations: With cutting-edge machinery sourced from leading global suppliers, we ensure minimal energy usage and low emission output across all our plants.
  • Product Excellence: Sustainability enhances our quality—every product is made to be more durable, safe, and reliable through green production techniques.
  • Promoting Circular Economy: Our PET and PP-based products are reprocessed into new forms, keeping valuable materials in use longer and reducing waste.

In addition to our recyclable material usage and lightweight packaging strategies, we support a healthier ecosystem through:

  • Efficient Resource Use: Our packaging designs use less raw material, helping conserve Earth’s natural resources.
  • Food Preservation: By extending shelf life and protecting contents, our products reduce food wastage in the supply chain.
  • Lower Emissions: Our sustainable packaging results in fewer greenhouse gases compared to conventional packaging materials.

We continuously research and evolve to introduce better materials and greener packaging practices that respect our planet.
